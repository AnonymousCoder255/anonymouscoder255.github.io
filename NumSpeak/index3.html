Division!
Division turns the divisor into adjective(s) describing the dividend. When written horizontally, it must be grouped like so:
1(+4)((5*5)/(5*4)).
I like (game/fun).
I like a fun game.

With that, you can decode the title: (1*6)((5*2)/5) meaning learn (language/number) or "Learn the Language Of Numbers (NumberSpeak)."

Division done vertically looks like this:

5*2 / 5

However, since I'm lazy, all (5*2)/5 will be in the horizontal division format.
