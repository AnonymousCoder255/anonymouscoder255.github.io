The radical radicals are quite rad(ical).

Square roots make questions; when simply square rooting a noun you can ask "what/which (noun)," so "where?" can be: 7 * 5 2

In similar fashion, "who?" can be: 9 * 2 2

However, by surrounding a whole sentence, you can make a yes-or-no question: 27 2 and this would mean "Are you fighting?"


Cube roots express wants, and they surround predicates.
For example, 1 5 * 9 3 "I want to have a rock."
